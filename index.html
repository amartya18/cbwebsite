About us

What does our logo represent?

With one hand representing a child and the other representing all the youth and adults, our logo simply symbolizes a helping hand towards all the children and adolescents who do not have any access to proper education. The color blue also represents sincerity in peace and equality.

More About us

Founded in September 2019, Handtohand now strives to achieve equality for education focusing on different regions of Indonesia
Existing as a social movement, we are here to create an impact on the youth by lending a helping hand.

Mission

Our vision is to reach a world in which every child receives equal rights regarding education. We strive and long to see our new generation working together without the divided hierarchies the society has placed today.

Our mission is to finally reach the dreams of the youth today. To rocket higher than our society ever has. We want to make sure that every child who does not have quality education, achieves it through our organization..
