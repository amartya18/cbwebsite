SDN SUKATANI
Today we headed over to SDN sukatani 2, a national school that is located along small roads that are hidden between the small housing areas. Together with @seedling.id we taught the students about deforestation and how important it is for us, the young generation, to protect our earth's environment The activities we had them do today included making recycled bracelets/necklaces as well as planting a couple of trees there at their school. In the class, we took the time to sit them down and explain just what deforestation is, how it works, how to prevent it, why prevent it and much more like so. It was memorable for both the children and our team.

SEKOLAH ASEMKA

Our second official visit was to Sekolah Belajar ASEMKA, right in the middle of what anyone would call nowhere! Despite studying on thin mattresses, rocky land and under a bridge where sitting straight was a struggle, the children studied with a fiery passion, continually asked questions and perpetually wanted to learn more.

SDN HARJAMUKTI
SDN Harjamukti 2 was HandtoHand’s first ever official visit. As a team, we visited Indonesian kids who don’t have the resources to learn the global language; English. It was amazing to see how determined the children were to get the English words right. Despite not having an English lesson, the children knew more than expected. We made a promise that day, both the children and our team, that despite the drawbacks that life may bring, our hope shall not fall back.

About Us

Here, we stand as individuals responsible for our future generation. We are those accountable for how we build our nation today. The complications that are caused by the lack of education are darkening our paths to a euphoric future.

What does our logo represent?

With one hand representing a child and the other representing all the youth and adults, our logo simply symbolizes a helping hand towards all the children and adolescents who do not have any access to proper education. The color blue also represents sincerity in peace and equality.

More About us

Founded in September 2019, Handtohand now strives to achieve equality for education focusing on different regions of Indonesia
Existing as a social movement, we are here to create an impact on the youth by lending a helping hand.

Vision

Our vision is to reach a world in which every child receives equal rights regarding education. We strive and long to see our new generation working together without the divided hierarchies the society has placed today.

Mission

Our mission is to finally reach the dreams of the youth today. To rocket higher than our society ever has. We want to make sure that every child who does not have quality education, achieves it through our organization.
